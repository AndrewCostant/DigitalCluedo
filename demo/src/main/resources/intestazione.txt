Cluedo (called Clue in North America) is a classic murder-mystery board game.
Players take on the roles of different characters and try to solve a crime by 
discovering who committed the murder, with which weapon, and in which room of the mansion.

Starting from the HALL, players move around the board, make suggestions, and gather clues 
by logically eliminating possibilities based on the cards shown by other players. 
The first player to correctly deduce the suspect, the weapon, and the location wins the game. 

Enjoy and have fun :)
